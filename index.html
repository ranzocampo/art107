Caged Dilemma portrays the current pandemic that all of us are facing today. Being isolated, quarantined, and socially distanced from others immensely help prevent humans from getting infected from COVID-19, but will they be safe internally? In this piece, I want to explore the idea of the external source influencing the internal. Being isolated has risks psychologically, physically and also microbially. With overwhelming exterior sources like what the news media displays about the virus, it can negatively impact a person’s mental health. Excess worrying can make an individual develop symptoms of anxiety and depression. The year 2020 has brought up a caged dilemma for all of us that makes us feel trapped inside, even though what we call a “home” is supposed to make us feel safe, it has become a danger to our well being.
